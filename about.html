About Me

Abdul-Quadri Ayomide Al-Ameen, popularly known as Sen. MiideOba, is an indigene of Oke-Ogun, Oyo State, Nigeria, born into the Al-Ameen family on September 19. He began his education at Muslim Association of Nigeria (MAN) Primary School, Okeho, before relocating to Lagos where he completed his primary education at Lad-Ikhlass Private School, Shasha, serving as the Social Prefect. He later graduated from MAN Secondary School, Egbeda, Lagos.

He is a recent graduate of Agricultural Extension and Rural Sociology from the prestigious Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ogun State, where he finished with a Second-Class Upper Division (CGPA 4.02).
